Welcome to the Halloween Haunt Ticket Booking System, a comprehensive web-based application that offers a seamless ticket booking experience for the Halloween Haunt event. Our platform incorporates robust security measures based on user authorization levels, ensuring that sensitive information is protected at all times. Using Spring Boot framework and Thymeleaf template engine for the front-end, and Spring Security for authentication and authorization, our platform offers a smooth and intuitive interface for users to purchase tickets after providing necessary details such as age, gender, student status, and pets preference. The system enables venders to manage tickets effectively, providing full control to add, view, edit, and delete tickets, while guests can only purchase their own tickets. Furthermore, venders have access to records and reports on ticket sales that are not available to guests. Our application is fully functional, secure, and user-friendly, making it the perfect solution to manage ticket sales for Halloween Haunt.
